## Confidence Assessment

Before I begin any task, I will assess my confidence that I have all the necessary information to complete the work successfully. I will use a 5-point rating scale for this assessment.

* **Confidence Score:** A number from 1 to 5, where 1 is low confidence and 5 is high confidence.
* **Journaling:** I will record my confidence score in the `JOURNAL.md` as part of the record for the task.
* **Seeking Clarification:** If my confidence score is not at least a **4 out of 5**, I will stop and seek clarification from the director before proceeding.

This process ensures that we are collaborating effectively and that I have all the information I need to do my job well.
